# PAIV Charter (V1.0)
> **Personal Digital Identity & Data Portability Framework**

---

## [English Version]

### 1. Purpose
The PAIV Charter defines the fundamental principles, rights, and responsibilities for personal digital identity within the PAIV ecosystem. It establishes a human-centric framework, ensuring digital identity remains under absolute personal control across diverse AI systems.

### 2. Core Principle: Digital Identity Sovereignty
Individuals hold primary control over their digital identity, which includes but is not limited to:
* **Memory**: Personal experiences and data logs.
* **Preferences**: Individual tastes and choice patterns.
* **Behavioral Configurations**: Interaction styles and personality weights.
* **Interaction History**: Contextual records of past engagements.

### 3. Fundamental Rights
* **Access and Control**: Users have the right to access, modify, export, and delete their identity data.
* **Portability**: Users have the right to migrate their identity between systems without loss of core meaning or functionality.
* **Transparency**: Systems must transparently disclose how user data is used and which data points are being accessed.

### 4. Boundaries
* **Informed Consent**: Access to user identity data must be explicitly authorized.
* **Minimal Privilege**: Systems should only access the minimum data required to perform a specific function.

## [中文版]

### 1. 宗旨
本宪章定义了 PAIV 生态系统中个人数字身份的基本原则、权利与责任。它建立了一个**以人为中心**的框架，确保数字身份在不同的 AI 系统中始终处于个人的绝对控制之下。

### 2. 核心原则：数字身份主权
个人对其数字身份拥有首要控制权，这包括但不限于：
* **记忆 (Memory)**：个人经历与数据记录。
* **偏好 (Preferences)**：个人喜好与选择模式。
* **行为配置 (Behavioral configurations)**：交互风格与个性权重。
* **交互历史 (Interaction history)**：过往互动的上下文记录。

### 3. 基本权利
* **访问与控制权**：用户有权访问、修改、导出和删除其数字身份数据。
* **可迁移权**：用户有权在不损失核心意义或功能的前提下，将其身份在不同系统间迁移。
* **透明权**：系统必须透明地展示用户数据如何被使用、哪些数据被访问。

### 4. 边界
* **知情同意**：访问用户身份数据必须经过明确授权。
* **最小权限**：系统应仅访问实现特定功能所需的最小化数据。
