# PAIV Governance Framework
> **Mechanism for Evolution and Community Contribution**

---

## [English Version]

### 1. Introduction
The PAIV (Personal AI Identity & Values) Protocol is an open-standard initiative. To ensure its long-term stability and adaptability, this framework defines how the protocol is maintained and updated.

### 2. PIP (PAIV Improvement Proposal)
Any major change to the protocol must be submitted as a **PIP**.
* **Draft**: Initial idea submitted for discussion.
* **Review**: Community and core maintainers evaluate the technical impact.
* **Accepted**: The proposal is integrated into the next protocol version.
* **Final**: The change is officially deployed.

### 3. Core Principles
* **User-Centric**: Every change must prioritize the user's data sovereignty.
* **Backward Compatibility**: New versions should avoid breaking existing implementations where possible.
* **Transparency**: All governance decisions are recorded publicly in this repository.

## [中文版]

### 1. 简介
PAIV (Personal AI Identity & Values) 协议是一项开放标准倡议。为了确保其长期稳定性和适应性，本框架定义了协议的维护与更新机制。

### 2. PIP (PAIV 改进提案)
任何对协议的重大更改都必须以 **PIP** 的形式提交。
* **草案 (Draft)**：提交讨论的初步想法。
* **评审 (Review)**：社区和核心维护者评估技术影响。
* **已接受 (Accepted)**：提案被整合进下一个协议版本。
* **定稿 (Final)**：更改正式发布。

### 3. 核心原则
* **用户中心**：每一次更改都必须优先考虑用户的数字主权。
* **向后兼容**：新版本应尽量避免破坏现有的系统实现。
* **透明化**：所有治理决策都公开记录在本仓库中。
