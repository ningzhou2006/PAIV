# PAIV Foundational Thesis
> **The Case for a Portable AI Identity Layer**

---

## [English Version]

### 1. The Core Problem: Fragmented Identity
[cite_start]Today, digital identity is locked inside siloed platforms[cite: 7, 100]. [cite_start]System A knows part of you, while System B knows another, forcing users to "re-introduce" themselves to every new AI assistant[cite: 5, 6, 11]. [cite_start]This creates platform lock-in and a loss of personal memory[cite: 112, 113].

### 2. The Vision: Digital Sovereignty
[cite_start]Humans now exist in the digital world through memory and interaction[cite: 139]. PAIV ensures this digital self remains:
* [cite_start]**Owned by the individual**, not the platform[cite: 141, 142].
* [cite_start]**Portable and Persistent** across agents, devices, and environments[cite: 102, 103].
* [cite_start]**Transparent**, allowing users to understand how AI uses their data[cite: 132, 133].

### 3. Positioning
[cite_start]PAIV is **not a product**, but a **protocol**[cite: 25, 147]. [cite_start]It aims to become the shared identity layer for the AI agent era—a continuity layer for people across diverse environments.

## [中文版]

### 1. 核心问题：碎片化的身份
[cite_start]目前，数字身份被锁定在孤岛化的平台中 [cite: 7, 100][cite_start]。系统 A 了解你的一部分，系统 B 了解另一部分，导致用户在面对每一个新 AI 助手时都必须“重新介绍”自己 [cite: 5, 6, 11][cite_start]。这造成了平台锁定和个人记忆的流失 [cite: 112, 113]。

### 2. 愿景：数字主权
[cite_start]人类正通过记忆与交互存在于数字世界中 [cite: 139]。PAIV 确保这一数字自我始终：
* [cite_start]**由个人所有**，而非平台掌控 [cite: 141, 142]。
* [cite_start]在不同智能体、设备和环境之间具有**可迁移性与持久性** [cite: 102, 103]。
* [cite_start]**透明化**，允许用户了解 AI 如何使用其数据 [cite: 132, 133]。

### 3. 定位
[cite_start]PAIV **不是一个产品**，而是一个**协议** [cite: 25, 147][cite_start]。它的目标是成为 AI 智能体时代的通用身份层——一个连接不同环境的人类行为连续层 。
